It has been a long time since I was hooked to a TV show, along came Heroes, this must be one of the best series I've seen, it's quite different from all the other series out there, it's an absolute suspense thriller. Lost had some good points about it but it surely started disappointing me after a while. Heroes so far has kept me glued. I have all the 21 episodes aired till date on my PC and I couldn't wait to see what happens next.
