TCL expects to launch its new compact desktop PC 'S-series' in the first half of next year in the China market. Besides the general computer specifications, the 'air purifying feature' and colorful design with white, black, and orange colors are noticeable features. The 22-inch monitor has a brightness-adjustable feature depending on different occasions like watching movies, playing games, or working on general documents.
