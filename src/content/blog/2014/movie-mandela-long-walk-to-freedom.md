Mandela Long Walk to Freedom is a biographical movie about Nelson Mandela. While I really applaud what Mandela represented in real life, I think that this movie puts him in bad light and I had to come back home and re-read about history. 
The focus of the movie went towards making Idris Elba trying to be the character, which at a lot of times is unconvincing (changes in accent, look) and not towards the storyline. The movie lacks coherence and even the ability to keep the viewer hooked. 
You know a movie is bad when all you want to do is take out your phone and play a Candy Crush(I know its a dumb game)
Naomi Harris is cast as Winnie Mandela and I think that the storyline makes her much more appealing.
The Casting is not good, Screenplay is shoddy and Storyline is so-so. 
> A disappointment to what could have been.

It was Long watch for the viewers
